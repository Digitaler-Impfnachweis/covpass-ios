Update: What is new?

This update provides bug fixes as well as new features.

  • Check vaccination status


    According to the German Infection Protection Act (IfSG), people are considered fully vaccinated if they are vaccinated at least three times or alternatively are vaccinated two times in addition to being recovered from COVID19 at least once. The vaccination status according to IfSG can now be checked with the CovPassCheck app.

  • Accessibility


    It's not perfect yet, but we have done a lot to improve the accessibility within the CovPass and CovPassCheck app.
